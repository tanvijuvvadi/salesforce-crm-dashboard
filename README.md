# 🚀 Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Dashboard in Salesforce

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the implementation of a complete Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solution using Salesforce Sales Cloud. It simulates how organizations manage customer interactions, sales pipelines, opportunities, accounts, leads, and customer support while enabling data-driven decision-making through reports and dashboards.

The project combines Salesforce Administration, CRM Analytics, SQL, Excel, and Business Intelligence concepts to build an end-to-end CRM solution.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with:

- Poor lead management
- Lack of visibility into sales performance
- Inefficient opportunity tracking
- Customer follow-up delays
- Limited reporting
- Inaccurate revenue forecasting
- Poor customer support tracking

This project addresses these challenges using Salesforce CRM.
